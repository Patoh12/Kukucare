Revolutionizing Poultry Farming with AI

Our cutting-edge AI solutions help farmers maximize productivity and animal welfare.

Smart Monitoring System

Our AI-powered sensors monitor chicken health, feed consumption, and environmental conditions in real-time, alerting you to potential issues before they become problems.

Predictive Analytics

Advanced algorithms analyze historical and real-time data to predict growth rates, optimal harvest times, and potential disease outbreaks, maximizing your farm's efficiency.

Veterinary AI Assistant

Our AI chatbot provides instant preliminary diagnosis of poultry health issues and recommends appropriate treatments, available 24/7 to all our partner farmers.

Automated Feeding

Smart feeding systems adjust portions based on chicken age, weight, and activity levels, reducing waste while ensuring optimal nutrition for your flock.

About Kuku Halisi Kenya

Founded in 2015, Kuku Halisi Kenya began as a small family poultry farm with a vision to transform Kenya's poultry industry through innovation and quality.

Today, we're a leading integrated poultry company combining traditional farming values with cutting-edge technology to deliver premium products while empowering farmers nationwide.

Our Mission

"To provide nutritious, high-quality poultry products to Kenyan families while revolutionizing poultry farming through technology and farmer empowerment."
